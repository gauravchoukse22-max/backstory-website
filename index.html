The problem

We're generous with grace for everyone except ourselves.

We tell ourselves things came easy. That other people have it figured out. That we haven't really been through much.

In reality, everyone has been shaped by real losses and real gains — moments that tested them and moments that carried them forward. We just rarely stop to look at the shape of that journey.

How it works

Your story, one milestone at a time.

Capture the days that mattered — the ups and the downs — and see the full story of how you got here.

1

Log your milestones

The wins and the losses, the firsts and the goodbyes — as they happen, or years later. Add the story, photos, even a voice memo.

2

Watch your story build

Backstory turns them into a personal timeline — the full shape of your journey, all in one private place.

3

Get them back, every year

On each milestone's day, Backstory brings the memory back to you — "3 years ago today" — so your life accumulates instead of slipping away.

The feature that matters most

Cherish, not just store.

Every year, Backstory notifies you about your memories from that time — so you don't just save them, you actually revisit them.

3 years ago today

This isn't a passive journal. It actively brings your past back to you at meaningful moments — not to dwell on the past, but to remember what you've already overcome.

Give yourself credit for what you've survived.

The hard days you got through. The good days you made happen. They're all part of one story — yours — and it deserves to be remembered.
